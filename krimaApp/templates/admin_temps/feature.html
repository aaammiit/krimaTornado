Here About All Features Of Our Project's

Login:The process of entering a username and password to access a system, application, or website.
Password: A secret sequence of characters used to authenticate a user's identity.
Username:A unique identifier chosen by a user to log in to a system, application, or website.
Session:A temporary and interactive information exchange between a user and a system, application, or website.
Token:A unique digital identifier issued to a user after successful authentication, used to verify their identity for subsequent requests.
Two-Factor (2FA): A security process that requires a user to provide two different authentication factors, such as a password and a Email Verification, to access a system or application.

Data Transformation:The process of converting data from one format to another, such as aggregating data, converting data types, or applying mathematical functions.
Data Filtering: The process of selecting a subset of data based on specific criteria, conditions, or rules to exclude unwanted or irrelevant data.
Data Sorting:The process of arranging data in a specific order, such as alphabetical, numerical, or chronological, to facilitate analysis or processing.
Data Scrubbing: The process of identifying and correcting errors, inconsistencies, or inaccuracies in data.
Data Validation: The process of checking data against a set of rules, constraints, or criteria to ensure it meets specific requirements or standards.
Data Normalization: The process of organizing data to minimize data redundancy and improve data integrity by dividing large tables into smaller, related tables.
Data Cleansing: The process of identifying and correcting errors, inconsistencies, or inaccuracies in data to improve its quality and reliability.

In Progress:The task or project is currently being worked on and is in an active state.
On Hold: The task or project has been paused or put on hold due to dependencies, issues, or other reasons.
Completed: The task or project has been finished and meets the required criteria or standards.
Pending:The task or project is awaiting approval, review, or feedback from others.
Cancelled: The task or project has been terminated or cancelled due to various reasons.
Download:The process of transferring data or files from a remote system, such as a server or website, to a local device, such as a computer or mobile device.
